User Data Deletion Instructions

Last Updated: July 2026

General Industries respects your data privacy. If you are a current or former employee, or an authorized contractor, and you wish to have your WhatsApp contact information completely removed from the GI Hub ERP alert system, please follow the steps below.

How to Request Data Deletion

Because GI Hub is a secure internal Enterprise application, users cannot delete their own profiles from the public web interface. To request data deletion, you must submit an email to the IT Administrator.

Email: johnsonandrew.j@outlook.com
Subject Line: WhatsApp Data Deletion Request - [Johnson Andrew]
Required Info: Please include your Employee ID and the exact phone number currently receiving WhatsApp alerts.

What Happens Next?

  • Verification: The IT Administrator will verify your identity against our internal HR records.
  • Removal: Within 48 hours of verification, your phone number will be permanently unlinked and purged from the GI Hub automated notification database.
  • Confirmation: You will receive an email confirming that you will no longer receive any WhatsApp routing messages from the GI Hub system.

Data Retention Note

Please note that while your active contact information will be deleted to stop future messaging, historical operational ledger entries (such as who physically approved a Delivery Note or consumed a material in the past) are retained securely in our internal audit logs for corporate compliance and accounting purposes.
