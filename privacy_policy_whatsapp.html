Privacy Policy for GI Hub

Last Updated: July 2026

General Industries ("we", "our", or "us") operates the GI Hub Enterprise Resource Planning (ERP) system, accessible at https://gi.giinventory.com. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information when you interact with our system, specifically regarding our WhatsApp Business API integration.

1. Purpose of the Application

GI Hub is a strictly internal operational tool used by General Industries staff. The WhatsApp integration is used solely to send automated, system-generated alerts to authorized employees (e.g., Delivery Notes, Purchase Order approvals, and Returnable Item reminders).

2. Data Collection & Usage

Through our WhatsApp integration, we process the following data:

  • Phone Numbers: Employee phone numbers stored securely in our internal database to route operational notifications.
  • Message Content: Automated outbound messages regarding inventory and logistics, and inbound replies from employees confirming receipt or status.

We do not collect data from the general public. All data is used strictly for internal business operations and logistics tracking.

3. Data Sharing & Third Parties

We do not sell, rent, or share any personal information or message data with external third parties for marketing or advertising purposes. Message routing is facilitated exclusively through Meta's secure Cloud API infrastructure.

4. Data Retention and Deletion

Data is retained only as long as necessary for operational auditing and compliance. Authorized employees can request the removal or update of their phone number from the GI Hub alert system at any time by contacting the system administrator.
